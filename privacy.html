Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Home Surge Protection Frisco ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or interact with our services.

Home Surge Protection Frisco is committed to respecting your privacy and protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit this website and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

This policy applies to information we collect:

It does not apply to information collected by any third-party websites that may be linked to or from this site. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including information that can be used to identify you personally ("personal information") as well as non-personal information about your interaction with the site.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Fill out a contact form or request a quote.
  • Send us an email or other communication.
  • Subscribe to updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (if offered).

This information may include:

  • Name and contact details (such as email address and phone number).
  • Property location or general service area.
  • Any other information you choose to provide in free-text fields.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage of the site. This information does not by itself identify you personally, but it may be associated with other information that could identify you. This may include:

  • IP address and approximate location.
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system.
  • Pages viewed, referring/exit pages, and the dates and times of your visits.
  • Clickstream data and usage patterns on the site.

This information is typically collected using cookies, log files, and similar tracking technologies, as described in the Cookie Policy section below.

We use the information we collect about you or that you provide to us for various business purposes, including to:

  • Communicate with you about updates, service information, or changes to this Privacy Policy.

Depending on your location, our legal basis for collecting and using your personal information may include:

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

Any third parties that receive your information are expected to protect it and use it only for the specific purposes for which it was shared.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze usage, and support certain site features.

4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences or previously viewed pages.

4.2 Types of Cookies We May Use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly and to enable basic features such as page navigation.
  • Performance and analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve its content, layout, and functionality.

4.3 Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings, including blocking or deleting cookies. If you choose to disable cookies, some portions of our website may not function as intended or may be less responsive.

For more information on how to manage cookies, please refer to your browser’s help documentation or settings menu.

5. Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from accidental loss and unauthorized access, use, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.
